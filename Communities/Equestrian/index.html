Equestrian

Kill International Equestrian Centre

Kill International Equestrian Centre is one of the best-equipped equestrian centres throughout Europe. It boasts state-of-the-art facilities with three indoor and two outdoor arena’s including the well-known Allianz Olympic Arena. Kill International hosts a variety of equestrian events throughout the year, including the Allianz Horse Championships, the Allianz Champions Of The Year and the Allianz End of Season Show.

Such an association both shows our commitment to the Irish Leisure Horse Industry and allows us to promote our excellent Horse & Rider insurance product amongst the horse riding community.
