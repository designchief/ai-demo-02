New to MyAllianz? Register Now

With MyAllianz you can:

  • Manage your policies online
    all in one place
  • Pay premiums by card or
    direct debit
  • Get instant access to all of
    your insurance documents
  • Easily request changes to
    any of your policies
  • Notify us of a claim and
    track the progress online
  • …all at a time that suits you!

Registration is easy, here’s how...

1. Register your policy
To sign up for MyAllianz, all you need is a valid email address, mobile phone number and your active policy/claim number. Enter these details and click register.

2. Activate your account
You'll then receive a confirmation email with a link, which must be clicked within 30 minutes. When you click on the link in the email an SMS will be sent to your mobile. Enter the code from the SMS within 30 minutes to complete the registration process.

3. Sign in and get going
Using your email address and password, you can access MyAllianz using the secure login and take control of your insurance at a time that suits you.
