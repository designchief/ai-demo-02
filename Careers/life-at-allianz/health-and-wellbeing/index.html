Health & Wellbeing

The health and wellbeing of our staff is very important for us at Allianz Ireland. Through an annual health and wellbeing month employees are encouraged to maintain a pro-active approach to a healthy lifestyle. Employees of Allianz Ireland are offered complementary health screenings as well as additional screenings to complement annual flue vaccines and VDU screenings.

In addition to our health and wellbeing month a number of wellness programmes are carried out throughout the year. These focus mainly on two areas, Mental and Physical health.

The financial industry is a very fast passed environment. Coping with an ever changing workload is a challenge for any individual. We have recently begun holding events relating to tackling stress and dealing with mental health issues.

Striking a balance between work and exercising can be tough. Therefore taking care of your physical health is particularly important. At Allianz we recognise that concentrating on your physical health is just as vital as maintaining your mental health. We empower employees to care for their health. Exercise programmes, from boot camps to yoga, were incorporated alongside workshops and seminars on topics such as smoking cessation, switching off technology, and several talks and briefings on nutrition and motivation all through the year.
