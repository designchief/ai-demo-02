Protected No Claims Bonus

Choosing to add Bonus Protection cover to your comprehensive policy ensures an accident doesn't have to be a disaster. If you add Bonus Protection this means that in any consecutive three-year renewal period you may incur one unlimited third party or accidental damage claim without this having an impact on your No Claim Bonus entitlement. In addition, any claim payment for windscreen breakage, fire or theft will be paid without impact on your No Claim Bonus entitlement.

Accident & Emergency Assistance

Our emergency assistance service is there 24/7/365 to offer advice and assistance should you have an accident or find that your car has been stolen or vandalised. We will get you home safely, tow your car to one of our approved garages and give you a free replacement car for up to 5 days while yours is being repaired. Or if your car is stolen we will give you a replacement car for up to 14 days at no extra cost.

Breakdown Assistance

Allianz Breakdown Assistance is available 24/7/365. Breakdown Assistance can be added to any private motor policy as an optional extra. It provides assistance if your car breaks down, won't start or need help if you have a puncture. It provides assistance if your car breaks down, won't start or has a puncture that you need help to fix. It complements our already established Motor Accident, Fire & Theft Emergency Service.

Benefits:

  • We will send a competent repairer to assist you at thebreakdown site.
  • We will cover the cost of towing your car to the nearest competent repairer or to your own garage, whichever is closest.
  • A free courtesy car for up to 48 hours.
