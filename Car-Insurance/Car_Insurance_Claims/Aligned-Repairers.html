Aligned Repairers

 

Benefits of the Aligned Repairer

If your car is damaged as a result of an accident or fire contact Allianz on 1890 779 999 (call operator charges may vary) to report the incident. We have an extensive panel of Allianz Aligned Repairers which you can chose from. If you decide to have your vehicle brought to one of our approved garages you will receive :

  • A Class A courtesy vehicle (Ford Ka or similar) for 5 days.
  • A three year warranty on all repair work carried out by the garage
  • Direct billing. If the garage repairs the vehicle all you have to do is pay your policy excess, we will take care of the rest.

If you decide to use another repairer you should obtain an estimate for repairs as soon as possible and forward it to us. However if you use another garage we cannot offer the same benefits to you.
